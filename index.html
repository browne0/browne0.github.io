About Me

Fun Fact #1  

The first time I started coding was the summer after I turned nine years old. Through an awesome opportunity going to a computer camp called SummerTech, I was able to learn the basics and continually grow my skills as a developer and a person.

Since then I've grown from creating simple websites with basic HTML and CSS, to creating complex, scalable, and functional applications for both web and mobile interfaces using platforms such as AWS complimented with DynamoDB as well as Apple's extensive iOS SDK complimented with Google's Firebase, or Parse.

Fun Fact #2

One thing that always interests people is that I'm half Jamaican, and half British. My mom is a native of Jamaica, and even though I was born in the U.S., I have a strong connection to Jamaica's culture thanks to her. To make things even more eclectic, my dad was born and raised in England until he turned 18 and moved to the U.S. with his dad!
