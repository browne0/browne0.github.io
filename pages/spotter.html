Spotter

Description: Landing page for local startup

View the live site

This website was the launch page for a local startup named Spotter when I was in college. The company had a goal to track fitness programs with their own custom bluetooth device.

Spotter already had a very basic template picked out for the project, but they needed assistance implementing some of the features on the website. I added two pages to the project, fixed bugs in the template, and changed the color and content to match the overall theme of the website.
